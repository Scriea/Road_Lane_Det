How to Use:


For images:	python3 LaneDetection.py --data <path of image> --type 1
for example: python LaneDetection.py --data ../data/1.jpg --type 1

For Video:	python3 LaneDetection.py --data <path of video> --type 2
for example: python LaneDetection.py --data ../data/1.mp4 --type 2
